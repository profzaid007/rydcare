Driver FAQs

Common questions from our driver community

How much can I earn as a RydCare driver?

Earnings depend on the number of hours you drive, the types of services you offer, and your location. Most of our full-time drivers earn competitive monthly incomes, and part-time drivers can supplement their earnings flexibly. We offer transparent per-hour and per-trip rates with no hidden cuts.

Can I choose my own working hours?

Absolutely. You decide when you're available to drive. Whether you want to work mornings, evenings, weekends, or only on specific days — it's entirely up to you. You can update your availability anytime through the driver app.

How and when do I get paid?

Payments are processed weekly and deposited directly into your bank account. You'll receive a detailed earnings breakdown every week showing trips completed, hours worked, and any bonuses earned. No delays, no surprises.

What does the verification process involve?

Our verification includes a background check, driving license validation, and a brief practical driving assessment. This typically takes 2-3 business days. We do this to ensure the safety of our customers and maintain the high quality of our service.

Do I need to own a car to drive with RydCare?

No, you don't need to own a vehicle. RydCare drivers are assigned to drive customers' vehicles. You'll be matched with rides based on your location and the type of vehicle you're comfortable driving. We also provide training on different vehicle types.

What support is available if I face issues on the road?

Our 24/7 driver support hotline is always available. Whether it's a navigation issue, customer concern, vehicle problem, or emergency — our team is just a call away. We also have an in-app chat feature for non-urgent queries.
