Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How quickly can I get a driver?

Our drivers typically arrive within 30 minutes for instant bookings. You can also schedule drivers in advance for future trips at your convenience.

Are your drivers verified and trained?

Yes, absolutely. All our drivers undergo comprehensive background checks, verification processes, and professional training. Safety is our top priority.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is transparent and based on factors like distance, duration, and service type. You'll see the estimated cost upfront before confirming your booking. No hidden charges.

Can I book a driver for multiple days?

Yes! We offer multi-day packages for outstation trips, events, and corporate needs. Contact our support team for custom packages tailored to your requirements.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

You can cancel bookings through our app or website. Cancellation charges may apply based on timing. Free cancellation is available up to 2 hours before scheduled pickup.

Do you operate in my city?

We currently operate in 25+ major cities across the country. Enter your location in our app to check availability in your area. We're constantly expanding to new cities!
